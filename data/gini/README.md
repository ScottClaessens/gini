**Reference:** Kohler, T. A., Bogaard, A., & Ortman, S. G. (2025). Introducing the special feature on housing differences and inequality over the very long term. *Proceedings of the National Academy of Sciences*, *122*(16), e2401989122. <https://doi.org/10.1073/pnas.2401989122>

**Source data URL:** <https://core.tdar.org/dataset/502392/siteginilevel>

**Date accessed:** 6th February 2026
